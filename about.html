About Us

Get to know us.

Our ultimate goal is to become the go-to house builder around Mthatha and keep our clients happy by building trustworthy homes that nurture confidence and bring families together under a solid shelter that will last for ages. Every house is a monument of freedom and we vouch to give yours the best quality at affordable prices. We are more than just builders - we are people on a mission to help families live better lives in better homes. We do all structural building works from foundation to roof so you can work less & be re-assured of a seamless house for less money.

Yah, we do that. And more!

History

The beginning of BuildUs is a himbling and inspiring story that embodies the power of unity and human spirt as 3 men mixed their blood, sweat and concrete to build their first home to a dazzling success that looks as good as the day we handed the keys back to a happy client.
That moment, that feeling and that curving smile of joy on their faces is a symbol of the power and drive that fuels our desire to keep doing what we do.

Vision

Our vision is to become a household name and the go-to builder trusted by communities all around Mthatha and the rest of the OR Tambo District Municipality.

Mission

We plan to keep the quality of the work we do as high as the day we started and ensure our clients nothing but the best quality on every surface of the home that we touch.
We don't compromise!
